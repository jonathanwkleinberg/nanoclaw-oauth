NanoClaw

NanoClaw is a personal AI assistant application that integrates with Google services to help its owner manage daily tasks.

What NanoClaw Does

NanoClaw connects to the following Google services on behalf of its authorized user:

  • Gmail — Read, search, and compose emails. NanoClaw helps manage your inbox by summarizing messages, drafting replies, and finding important emails.
  • Google Calendar — View, create, update, and manage calendar events. NanoClaw helps with scheduling, finding free time, and keeping track of upcoming events across personal and shared calendars.

Who Uses NanoClaw

NanoClaw is a private, single-user application built for personal use. It is not available to the general public. Only the application owner authorizes access to their own Google account.

Privacy

NanoClaw does not share, sell, or transmit any user data to third parties. All data is processed locally on the user's own device. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
