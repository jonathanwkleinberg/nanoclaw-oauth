Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

What this app does

NanoClaw is a private, personal-use application that accesses Google Gmail and Google Calendar on behalf of its sole authorized user.

Data collected

This application accesses Gmail messages and Google Calendar events only when explicitly requested by the authorized user. No data is shared with third parties, sold, or used for advertising.

Data storage

OAuth credentials (access and refresh tokens) are stored locally on the user's own device. No data is stored on external servers.

Data sharing

None. This is a single-user application. No data is transmitted to any third party.
